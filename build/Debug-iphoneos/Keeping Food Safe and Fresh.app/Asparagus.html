Selection:

  • Fresh, tender, firm, fairly straight stalks with close compact tips

Storage Location:

  • Refrigerator, wrap the asparagus stem ends in a wet paper towel and put them in a plastic bag or stand them up with the stem in a little water; freezer, blanched or cooked

Storage Length:

  • Refrigerator: 3 - 4 days
  • Freezer: 8 months

Availability:

  • August to June; peak from April to May

Others:

  • Use quickly, there is no benefit from storage
